Wattwise Policies

Simple project policies for how Wattwise presents information, references, and user privacy.

Privacy Policy

Wattwise is an educational awareness website. It does not require users to share private household electricity bills, passwords, or sensitive personal information to learn from the site.

Terms of Service

Wattwise provides energy awareness, comparison, and practical action ideas for learning. Users should treat the content as educational guidance, not professional engineering advice.

Data Privacy

Wattwise uses public reference data for awareness. If users contact the project team, shared messages should stay respectful and avoid unnecessary sensitive information.
