2040 Outlook

A Future Yangon With Smarter Energy Choices

The 2040 outlook is about future change, not a fixed prediction. If we keep improving awareness, daily habits, technology choices, and community planning, energy use can become more responsible across homes, schools, shops, and transport.

Wattwise connects today's energy habits with tomorrow's possibilities: less avoidable waste, stronger local action, and a city that is more prepared for sustainable living.

01

Energy-aware daily life

People make electricity use part of everyday decision-making, from charging devices to cooling rooms and using appliances.

02

Smarter homes and schools

Classrooms, households, and small businesses follow better routines, reduce standby waste, and choose efficient equipment.

03

Cleaner technology choices

Renewable energy, smart devices, and low-impact transport become easier to understand, compare, and use in daily life.

04

Community-level improvement

Local groups share practical actions, compare progress, and support energy habits that fit Yangon's real challenges.

Energy Impact Indicators

Daily energy habits Small actions

Small actions like leaving chargers plugged in, overusing lights, or running appliances unnecessarily can increase avoidable electricity waste over time.

Household & school efficiency Better routines

Energy-saving routines in homes and classrooms, including switching off devices, using efficient lighting, and reducing standby power, can lower unnecessary consumption.

Smarter consumption choices Long-term impact

Choosing LED lighting, efficient appliances, and better transport habits supports long-term energy efficiency in Yangon.
